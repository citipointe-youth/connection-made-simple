Youth Connection

Add to your Home Screen

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

1

Open connection-made-simple.vercel.app in Safari (not Chrome or Firefox).

2

Tap the Share button — the box with an arrow pointing up at the bottom of the screen.

3

Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen".

4

Tap Add in the top-right corner. The app icon will appear on your Home Screen.

Android (Chrome)

1

Open connection-made-simple.vercel.app in Chrome.

2

Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner of Chrome.

3

Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app".

4

Tap Add or Install to confirm. The app icon will appear on your Home Screen.
